Exercises by Muscle Group

Chest

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Focus on controlled movements to build pectoral muscles. Warm up with light cardio.

Back

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 5-10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Strengthen your lats and traps for better posture and pulling power.

Legs

  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Build quads, hamstrings, and glutes for overall lower body strength.

Shoulders

  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Target deltoids for broader shoulders and improved upper body aesthetics.

Arms (Biceps)

  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Chin-ups: 3 sets of 5-8 reps

Isolate biceps for arm definition and pulling strength.

Arms (Triceps)

  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Overhead Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Work triceps for arm balance and pushing power.

Core

  • Planks: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps per side

Strengthen abs and obliques for stability and a toned midsection.

Diet Plans

Fat Loss Plan

Aim for a calorie deficit (e.g., 500 calories below maintenance). Focus on whole foods, high protein, and veggies.

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and a scoop of protein powder (300 cal)
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and olive oil dressing (400 cal)
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and broccoli (500 cal)
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt and almonds (200 cal total)
  • Total Daily Calories: ~1400 (adjust based on needs)

Weight Gain Plan

Aim for a calorie surplus (e.g., 500 calories above maintenance). Emphasize carbs, proteins, and healthy fats.

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with peanut butter, banana, and eggs (500 cal)
  • Lunch: Turkey sandwich with avocado, cheese, and whole grain bread (600 cal)
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with rice, veggies, and soy sauce (700 cal)
  • Snacks: Protein shake and trail mix (400 cal total)
  • Total Daily Calories: ~2200 (adjust based on needs)
